Mold Level 1

A damp cloth and detergent are sufficient, or natural mold cleaners like white vinegar can work.

Mold Level 2

Will require using a HEPA vacuum and cleaning all surfaces with a damp cloth.

Mold Level 3

Contact the mold Team. You are not allowed to clean it since you would need to be level 2 Certified, since it would be considered harmful to the person cleaning it if they don't have the proper chemicals and PPE.
